Vendor Management System

This project revolves around the development of a Volunteer Management System (VMS) database for the National Volunteer Initiative (NVI). As a Non-Government Organization that aims to promote volunteerism in Singapore, NVI is seeking a VMS that can facilitate the matching of potential volunteers with organizations that require their assistance.

The project's main objective is to create a VMS database capable of supporting the essential features specified in the project document.
